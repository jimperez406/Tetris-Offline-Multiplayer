Tetris Game Instructions

Welcome to Tetris! Here's how to play:

Objective: Clear as many lines as possible by fitting the falling blocks together.

Controls:

  • D \ Left Arrow: Move block left
  • A \ Right Arrow: Move block right
  • S \ Down Arrow: Move block down faster
  • Q \ Up Arrow /Shift: Rotate block

Scoring:

  • Single Line Clear: 10 points
  • Double Line Clear: 20 points
  • Triple Line Clear: 30 points
  • Tetris (Four Line Clear): 40 points

Game Over: The game ends when the blocks reach the top of the screen. Aim for a high score!

Enjoy playing Tetris!
